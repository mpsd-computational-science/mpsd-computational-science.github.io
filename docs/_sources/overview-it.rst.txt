Overview IT services
--------------------

The provision of IT-related services for MPSD is split between

- `GWDG <http://gwdg.de>`_

- `DESY <https://it.desy.de/index_eng.html>`_

- `MPCDF <http://mpcdf.mpg.de>`_

- `MPDL <http://mpdl.mpg.de>`_

Additional notes are available from the SSU Computational Science at `http://mpsd-computational-science.github.io <http://mpsd-computational-science.github.io>`_ .
