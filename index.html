Computational Science SSU¶





Welcome

You are looking at the home page of the Computational Science scientific support unit (SSU) at the Max Planck Institute for Structure and Dynamics of Matter (MPSD) in Hamburg, Germany.

A growing amount of information is available in our resources section, including:

  • Overview IT-related services

  • Relevant training and seminars (Events)

  • Advise on Research Data Management, Archiving research data, Keeper

  • Documentation on High Performance Computing (HPC)

Please get in touch (Contact) with your queries - the materials are developed in response to your needs.
